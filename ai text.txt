Artificial Intelligence (AI) is a field of computer science that focuses on building systems that can perform tasks that normally require human intelligence.

AI is used in healthcare to help doctors diagnose diseases more accurately and quickly.

In finance, AI is used for fraud detection and risk analysis.

Machine Learning is a subset of AI that allows systems to learn from data without being explicitly programmed.

Deep Learning is a type of Machine Learning that uses neural networks with many layers.

AI is also used in self-driving cars, virtual assistants, and recommendation systems.
